## About

Constantinople delivers a new era of AI-native banking. Constantinople provides banks with a fully-managed software and AI operational platform, which brings together customer experience, product, data, operations, compliance and infrastructure in a single platform.

Constantinople's AI-native platform eliminates the need for expensive infrastructure, and replaces manual operational and compliance processes with AI at scale. By removing the operational complexity of banking, Constantinople enables our client banks to focus on their customers and the business of banking.

## The Role

As a Senior Software Engineer at Constantinople, you will be a valuable technical leader, responsible for designing, developing, and delivering complex software solutions that form the foundation of our banking platform. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to build software that sets new industry standards for reliability, scalability, and security.

## What You'll Do

- Be an active contributor to cross-team initiatives and provide support to the engineering team
- Develop and drive architectural changes across projects while leading engineers
- Develop and maintain critical platform features
- Improve tooling to enable data-driven analysis and reporting to the wider Constantinople team
- Support a secure and functional platform
- Provide decisions on service performance and design implementation

## Who You Are

- Deep understanding of software design principles and scalable systems architecture
- Hands-on experience with Node.js and TypeScript in production environments
- Familiarity with frontend technologies (React / React Native) is a strong plus
- Experience building and owning features end-to-end, from idea to production
- Experience in serverless AWS architectures is highly desirable
- Exposure to Domain-Driven Design or event-driven systems is a bonus
- A collaborative mindset with experience mentoring and leading engineering initiatives
- Experience in fintech or regulated environments is helpful but not required
