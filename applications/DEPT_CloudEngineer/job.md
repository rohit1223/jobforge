## About DEPT®

DEPT® is a Growth Invention company built to help the world's most ambitious
brands grow faster. The organization operates at the intersection of technology
and marketing, with over 4,000 specialists delivering services across Brand &
Media, Experience, Commerce, CRM, and Technology & Data. Key clients include
Google, Lufthansa, Meta, eBay, and OpenAI. DEPT® achieved B Corp certification
and Climate Neutral status in 2021.

## Role overview

This position belongs to the Experience & Engineering team in the Netherlands,
combining technical development with design expertise. The team serves clients
such as Gant, Inter Milan, bol.com, and Just Eat Takeaway. The Cloud Engineer
builds cloud platforms and maintains solutions through automation and smart
workflows.

## Primary responsibilities

- Lead the infrastructure implementation/migration process from sprint 0 to go-live
- Collaborate to support smooth integration and long-term operational efficiency
- Establish best practices and deliver high-quality work
- Collaborate with client teams and developers to facilitate communication and ensure seamless transitions
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues that may arise during the implementation/migration

## Required qualifications

- Previous Cloud Engineer experience or similar role
- Multi-cloud platform experience (Microsoft Azure, AWS, and/or GCP)
- DevOps tooling expertise: Azure DevOps, GitHub, Datadog, Cloudflare
- Cloud Security & Networking understanding
- Infrastructure-as-Code experience (i.e. Terraform)
- Project ownership capability and ambition
- Working English proficiency
- Dutch proficiency advantageous but not required
