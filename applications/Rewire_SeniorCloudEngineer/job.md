### Who we are

AI is transforming the world – and we're just getting started. With data growing exponentially, the need for smart, impact-driven minds has never been greater. At Rewire, we don't just follow trends in Data & AI – we shape them.

If you're passionate about solving real challenges and creating tangible outcomes, then this is the perfect opportunity for you! With your innovative mindset and expertise, you're perfectly poised to help shape the future of Rewire, leveraging data and AI to develop cutting-edge solutions.

We're on a mission to grow Rewire's business by leaps and bounds, and we're looking for a **Senior Cloud Engineer** to help us reach our goals. In this role, you will take ownership of data & AI projects, collaborate as a peer with Cloud, Software, Data, and AI Engineers, and drive the design and delivery of production-grade cloud solutions for our clients.

You'll build the backbone of our cloud platforms and our clients' cloud environments by designing secure, scalable infrastructure and automating everything you touch. Be part of a dynamic team of data & AI talents who tackle meaningful challenges, build smart solutions, and have fun along the way. **Wanna join the ride?**

### Where you'll make a difference

- **Own** and architect cloud foundations that power digital transformations – design and implement the core infrastructure, platforms, and services that enable scalable products in AWS, Azure, or GCP.
- **Collaborate** across disciplines – work closely with fellow engineers, AI Experts and business transformation leaders as a technical peer to deliver production-ready platforms that create real impact for our clients
- **Enable** AI and event-driven systems at scale – design, deploy and evolve CI/CD pipelines, serverless architectures, networking, security, and the infrastructure that ML/AI workloads run on.
- **Design** and maintain high-quality, reusable infrastructure code – using Terraform, Python, and modern cloud tooling to automate everything from environments to deployments with reliability and scalability in mind
- **Translate** architecture into business value – explain cloud decisions clearly to stakeholders so teams understand why a design matters, not just how it works
